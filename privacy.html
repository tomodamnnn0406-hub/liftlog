🏋️ Lift Log — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 10 June 2026

Lift Log is a free, personal workout-tracking app. This policy explains what data the app handles and how. The short version: your data stays on your device and, if you choose, in your own Google Drive. It is never sent to us or sold to anyone.

What the app stores

When you use Lift Log, the following is saved locally on your device (in your browser's storage):

  • Your workouts — exercises, sets, weights, reps, dates, and notes
  • Your goals, personal records, and saved workout templates
  • Optional profile details you enter, such as height and body weight
  • Your preferences (units, language)

This information is created and controlled entirely by you. We do not operate a server, we have no user accounts, and we cannot see any of this data.

Google Drive backup (optional)

If you turn on Automatic cloud backup, you sign in with your Google account and the app saves a single backup file to a private application folder in your own Google Drive.

The app requests only the Google Drive Application Data permission (drive.appdata). This scope is strictly limited: the app can read and write only its own backup file and cannot see, open, or modify any of your other Google Drive files.

  • The backup contains the same workout data described above.
  • It is stored in your Drive, under your Google account — not ours.
  • We never receive, access, or store your Google data on any server.
  • Google's handling of your account is governed by Google's own Privacy Policy.

Lift Log's use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. Your Google Drive data is used only to provide the backup and restore feature, is not transferred to others, and is not used for advertising.

Optional feedback

If you choose to send feedback from within the app, the rating and any comments or email address you provide are submitted to a private Google Form so we can improve the app. This is entirely optional — the app works fully without it.

What we do NOT do

  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties.
  • We do not use your data for advertising.
  • We do not run analytics or tracking on your workouts.

Your control

  • Delete local data: clearing the app's site data in your browser removes everything stored on the device.
  • Stop cloud backup: tap "Disconnect Google Drive" in the app's settings.
  • Revoke access: you can remove Lift Log's access at any time at myaccount.google.com/permissions.
  • Delete the cloud backup: remove the app's hidden data in your Google Drive account settings.
